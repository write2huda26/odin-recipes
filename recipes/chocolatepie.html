Chocolate Pie

Description

This chocolate pecan pie is the perfect way to finish your holiday meal with a bang. Full of rich nutty flavor and chocolatey goodness, this decadent dessert will quickly become a tasty tradition.

Ingredients

  • Corn Syrup
  • sugar
  • Margarine
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Pecans
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Make the filling: Beat corn syrup, sugar, melted margarine, eggs, and salt in a mixing bowl until well-combined. Stir in the pecans and chocolate chips.
  2. Bake the pie: Pour the filling into the unbaked crust. Bake in a preheated oven until the filling is set. Let the pie cool before serving.
